Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for business plans.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be prorated based on your billing cycle.

How many devices can I use my license on?

+

It depends on your plan. Basic allows 2 devices, Premium allows 5 devices, and Business allows unlimited devices.

What's the difference between per-program and bundle licensing?

+

Per-program licensing gives you access to a single application (like Spec Document Manager) at the listed price. Bundle licensing gives you access to ALL current and future programs for a discounted rate, saving up to 40%.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

+

Your license will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll lose access to premium features.

Do you offer refunds?

+

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
